What is Cannabis Insider?

Billions of dollars are at stake in a market so lucrative that entrepreneurs everywhere are eyeing New Jersey. It’s an economic opportunity unrivaled, but obstacles loom large.

Our team of renowned journalists from NJ.com and The Star-Ledger will inform you about the issues, policies, laws, regulations, politics and people making an impact on the cannabis industry, community and marketplace.
